History of Activism at Wesleyan University

A historical archival project created in collaboration with Wesward, the Hermes, the University Organizing Center (UOC), Wesleying, and many more.

This project seeks to document Wesleyan University's activism history from 1968 to 2008. Information for this project is gathered from the Hermes's Wesleyan activism poster.
